Student Guide ​

This guide covers all the features and functionality available to students in the Learning Management System.

Dashboard ​

When you log in as a student, you'll see your personalized dashboard showing:

  • Your enrolled courses
  • Upcoming assignments and deadlines
  • Recent announcements
  • Your calendar with scheduled events

Courses ​

Viewing Your Courses ​

Your courses are displayed on the dashboard. Each course card shows:

  • Course name and code
  • Instructor information
  • Recent activity
  • Progress indicators

Accessing Course Materials ​

To access a course:

  1. Click on the course card from your dashboard
  2. Navigate through the course modules in the sidebar
  3. Click on any module to view its contents

Course materials may include:

  • Lecture notes and slides
  • Video content
  • Reading materials (PDFs, documents)
  • External links and resources

Course Files ​

You can download course files provided by your instructor:

  1. Navigate to the Files section in your course
  2. Browse or search for files
  3. Click the download button to save files to your device

Assignments ​

Viewing Assignments ​

Assignments are listed in:

  • The Assignments section of each course
  • Your calendar
  • Your dashboard (for upcoming deadlines)

Submitting Assignments ​

To submit an assignment:

  1. Navigate to the assignment
  2. Read the assignment instructions carefully
  3. Upload your submission file(s)
  4. Click "Submit" to finalize your submission

TIP

You can save drafts before submitting. Make sure to click "Submit" when you're ready for grading!

Checking Grades ​

Once graded, you can view:

  • Your score
  • Instructor feedback
  • Grade breakdown (if applicable)

Quizzes ​

Taking Quizzes ​

To take a quiz:

  1. Navigate to the quiz from your course
  2. Read the instructions and time limits
  3. Answer all questions
  4. Review your answers before submitting
  5. Click "Submit Quiz" when complete

WARNING

Pay attention to time limits! Quizzes may be timed, and you'll be automatically submitted when time expires.

Quiz Types ​

The LMS supports various question types:

  • Multiple choice
  • True/False
  • Short answer
  • Essay questions
  • Fill in the blank

Viewing Quiz Results ​

After submission:

  • Some quizzes show immediate results
  • Others require instructor grading
  • Check your grades section for quiz scores and feedback

Discussions ​

Participating in Discussions ​

To participate in course discussions:

  1. Navigate to the Discussions section
  2. Browse existing discussion threads
  3. Click "New Discussion" to start a new topic
  4. Reply to existing discussions to join the conversation

Discussion Etiquette ​

  • Be respectful and professional
  • Stay on topic
  • Contribute meaningfully to discussions
  • Follow your instructor's guidelines

AI Chat Assistant ​

The LMS includes an AI-powered chat assistant to help you:

  • Understand course concepts
  • Get study tips and guidance
  • Find resources within your courses
  • Answer questions about course materials

INFO

The AI assistant is a support tool. Always verify important information with your instructor.

Calendar ​

Using the Calendar ​

Your calendar displays:

  • Assignment due dates
  • Quiz availability windows
  • Course events
  • Personal reminders

You can:

  • View by day, week, or month
  • Filter events by course
  • Add personal events
  • Set reminders for important dates

Progress Tracking ​

Viewing Your Progress ​

Track your progress in each course:

  • Completion percentage for modules
  • Grades on assignments and quizzes
  • Overall course grade
  • Time spent on course activities

Analytics ​

View detailed analytics about your learning:

  • Activity over time
  • Content engagement metrics
  • Comparison with course averages (if enabled)
  • Areas needing attention

Profile and Settings ​

Managing Your Profile ​

Update your profile information:

  1. Click on your profile icon
  2. Select "Profile Settings"
  3. Update your information
  4. Save changes

You can update:

  • Display name
  • Profile picture
  • Contact information
  • Notification preferences

Notification Settings ​

Customize how you receive notifications:

  • Email notifications
  • In-app notifications
  • Frequency preferences
  • Types of notifications

Tips for Success ​

  • Check your dashboard daily for new announcements
  • Review assignment deadlines regularly
  • Participate actively in discussions
  • Utilize the AI chat for quick help
  • Keep track of your progress
  • Submit assignments before the deadline
  • Ask your instructor if you need clarification

Troubleshooting ​

Common Issues ​

Can't access a course:

  • Verify you're enrolled in the course
  • Check with your instructor if enrollment is pending

File upload issues:

  • Check file size limits
  • Ensure file format is supported
  • Try a different browser

Quiz problems:

  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Don't navigate away during a timed quiz
  • Contact your instructor if technical issues occur

Getting Help ​

If you encounter issues:

  1. Check this documentation
  2. Use the AI chat assistant
  3. Contact your course instructor
  4. Reach out to system administrators for technical issues
